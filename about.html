About Bookqart

The home for avid readers,book clubs,and the book obsessed

Do your friends turn to you first for book recommendations? Have you ever forgotten your keys but remembered to
grab a book when you left the house? Do you plan your life around your book club meetings?
You might be Bookoholic. Join the bookqart club!

Bookqart is an editorial content site connecting readers with books and authors. Our goal is to help readers discover
their next favorite book, and to help book clubs discover their next read. Features include author interviews and
exclusive essays, book club recommendations, reading challenges (like Bookish Bingo!), giveaways, and more.
No genre is off limits here. No matter what you like to read, we’ve got you covered.

For book clubs, we’ll get you ready for your next meeting with exclusive reading group content like discussion
guides, quizzes and games, food and drink suggestions, playlists, and more.

We believe in the power of reading, the importance of literacy, and the fact that books should be as inclusive and
diverse as readers are. Being Bookqart member is part of our identity, and it’s one we’re proud to have and share with you.

Be You, Be Bookqart.
